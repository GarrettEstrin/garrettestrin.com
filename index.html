About Garrett

I am a web developer and former grocery store manager that learned how to lead teams to a shared goal through focusing efforts and recognizing great ideas from any source. I am passionate about technology, learning and creating and have used these passions to expand on my skills in programming and web development.

My degree in professional management taught me to lead and be a part of teams that are comprised of individuals with individual needs and personalities.

At WDI, I was taught how to learn the latest and most in-demand programming languages quickly and to implement them efficiently in projects.

Web programming started as a hobby for me through building fun, personal websites and exploring what is possible in web programming. I am ready to turn this hobby into a career that I love.

I am currently looking for a full-time position that will allow me to use my programming and management skills.
